# Static Polymorphism — Links

Curated references for deeper exploration of CRTP and compile-time
polymorphism in C++.

- **[Curiously Recurring Template Pattern — cppreference.com](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/language/crtp)**
  The official reference page covering CRTP syntax and semantics.

- **[CRTP — fluent{C++}](https://www.fluentcpp.com/2017/05/12/curiously-recurring-template-pattern/)**
  A beginner-friendly multi-part series walking through motivation,
  mechanics, and practical examples of CRTP.

- **[Static Polymorphism with CRTP — foonathan::blog()](https://www.foonathan.net/2020/10/crtp/)**
  A modern look at CRTP including comparisons with C++20 concepts as an
  alternative constraint mechanism.

- **[CppCon 2021 — Back to Basics: Templates (Bob Steagall)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XN319NYEOcE)**
  A thorough conference talk covering template fundamentals, including
  CRTP and static dispatch patterns.

- **[Dynamic vs Static Polymorphism — Modernes C++](https://www.modernescpp.com/index.php/dynamic-and-static-polymorphism)**
  Side-by-side comparison of virtual dispatch and CRTP with performance
  measurements.

- **[C++ Core Guidelines: T.80 — Do not naively templatize a class hierarchy](https://isocpp.github.io/CppCoreGuidelines/CppCoreGuidelines#Rt-hier)**
  Official guidelines on when template-based hierarchies are appropriate
  and when they are not.
