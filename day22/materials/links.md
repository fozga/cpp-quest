# C++20 Concepts — Links & Resources

The following references cover C++20 concepts and constrained templates in depth.

- [Constraints and concepts — cppreference.com](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/language/constraints)
  Comprehensive reference for concept definitions, requires clauses, requires expressions, and constraint ordering.

- [Concepts library — cppreference.com](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/concepts)
  Overview of the standard library concepts defined in `<concepts>`, including `std::integral`, `std::floating_point`, and `std::regular`.

- [C++20 Concepts — A Quick Introduction — Bartek's Coding Blog](https://www.bfilipek.com/2021/02/cpp20-concepts-practical-approach.html)
  Beginner-friendly walkthrough covering concept syntax, requires clauses, and practical examples.

- [Simplify Your Code with C++20 Concepts — Modernes C++](https://www.modernescpp.com/index.php/c-20-concepts-the-details)
  Detailed article comparing SFINAE and concepts with side-by-side examples.

- [C++20 Concepts Are Structural — Arthur O'Dwyer](https://quuxplusone.github.io/blog/2020/07/22/concepts-are-structural/)
  Insightful blog post exploring the structural nature of concepts and their implications for overload resolution.

- [CppCon: Using C++20 Concepts (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=cppcon+c%2B%2B20+concepts)
  Conference talks demonstrating concept-driven design patterns and migration from SFINAE.
