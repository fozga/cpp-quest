# C++20 Ranges — Links

Supplementary resources for understanding the C++20 Ranges library.

- [cppreference — Ranges library](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/ranges) —
  Authoritative reference for all range concepts, views, and algorithms.
- [cppreference — `<ranges>` header](https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/header/ranges) —
  Complete listing of view adaptors and range utilities.
- [C++20 Ranges — A Complete Guide (Nicolai Josuttis)](https://www.josuttis.com/cpp20/ranges.html) —
  In-depth walkthrough of ranges, views, and sentinels from the author of
  *C++20 — The Complete Guide*.
- [Barry Revzin — "A Beginner's Guide to C++20 Ranges"](https://hannes.hauswedell.net/post/2019/11/30/range_intro/) —
  Gentle introduction with motivating examples.
- [Tristan Brindle — ranges-v3 to C++20 migration](https://tristanbrindle.com/posts/ranges-in-cpp20) —
  Practical notes on what shipped in C++20 vs. the original ranges-v3
  library.
- [Jason Turner — C++ Weekly: C++20 Ranges](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=c%2B%2B+weekly+ranges) —
  Short video episodes covering individual range features.
