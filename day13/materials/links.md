# Type-Safe Unions — Links

Curated resources for deeper understanding of `std::variant`, `std::visit`,
and discriminated-union patterns in modern C++.

- **cppreference — std::variant**
  <https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/utility/variant>
  Comprehensive reference for constructors, accessors, and helper functions.

- **cppreference — std::visit**
  <https://en.cppreference.com/w/cpp/utility/variant/visit>
  Specification and examples for single- and multi-variant visitation.

- **Bartlomiej Filipek — "Everything You Need to Know About std::variant"**
  <https://www.bfilipek.com/2018/06/variant.html>
  Thorough tutorial covering construction, access, visitation, and real-world
  use cases.

- **Bartlomiej Filipek — "2 Lines Of Code and 3 C++17 Features: The Overload Pattern"**
  <https://www.bfilipek.com/2019/02/2lines3featuresoverload.html>
  Explains the `overloaded` helper struct and how aggregate init, variadic
  using-declarations, and CTAD combine to make it work.

- **Jason Turner (C++ Weekly) — "std::variant" (Ep 134)**
  <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3wm5QzdddYc>
  Short video introduction to variant basics and common pitfalls.

- **Modernes C++ — "Visiting a std::variant with the Overload Pattern"**
  <https://www.modernescpp.com/index.php/visiting-a-std-variant-with-the-overload-pattern/>
  Walkthrough of building visitors with the overload pattern in C++17 and
  C++20.
